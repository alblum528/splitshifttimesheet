About Calculate My Hours

Providing free browser-based tools that make people's lives easier.

What We Do

Calculate My Hours is working to create tools for people that want easy ways to do repetitive tasks. We're focused on making easy to use webapps that can be used use over and over again. This free split-shift timesheet runs entirely in your browser. There's no account to create, no app to install, and no data sent to any server. You enter your hours, the tool does the math, and everything stays on your device.

It's designed for workers who clock in and out more than once a day — up to three separate shifts per day — and need an accurate, printable record of their time for payroll or tax purposes.

Who It's Built For

Split shifts are common across a wide range of jobs. Calculate My Hours is a good fit for:

  • Home health aides and personal care workers
  • Nurses, CNAs, and other healthcare staff
  • Restaurant, hospitality, and food service workers
  • Gig workers and independent contractors (Uber, DoorDash, Instacart, etc.)
  • Freelancers and hourly employees juggling multiple clients or shifts

If your schedule doesn't fit neatly into a single clock-in and clock-out, this tool was made for you.

Your Privacy

All timesheet data is saved in your browser's local storage. Nothing you enter is ever transmitted to a server, stored in a database, or shared with any third party. Clearing your browser's local storage will erase your data — it exists only on your device.

How We Keep It Free

Calculate My Hours is supported by Google AdSense advertising. Ads are served automatically by Google based on general context and your browsing preferences — we do not control which ads appear, and we do not receive any personal information about you from ad impressions. If you find the ads intrusive, most modern browsers support ad-free browsing via extensions.

Questions or Feedback?

We're a small independent project and we read every message. If something isn't working, you have a feature request, or you just want to say hello — reach out via our contact page.
