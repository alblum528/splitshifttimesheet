Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

This page explains what information Calculate My Hours collects, how cookies and advertising work on this site, and what choices you have. The short version: your spreadsheet and calculator data never leave your browser — the only information we or our partners collect is standard site analytics and advertising cookies, described below.

Your calculation data is never collected

Both calculators on this site — the Bulk Spreadsheet Calculator and the Single Timespan Calculator — run entirely in your browser using JavaScript and the SheetJS library. Any file you upload, and any date or time you type in, is processed locally on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers, never sent to a third party, and never stored in a database. Closing the tab clears everything.

🔒 Calculator inputs and uploaded files are 100% client-side

Information we collect

Analytics data

We use Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics (GA4) to understand aggregate traffic patterns — which pages are visited, how long people stay, what device and browser they're using, and which site referred them here. This is collected automatically for every visitor and is not tied to your name or email unless you separately contact us.

Contact form submissions

If you use the contact form, the name, email address, topic, and message you provide are sent to and processed by our form provider, Formspree, and delivered to us by email. We use this only to respond to your message.

Advertising data

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to collect data about your visits to this and other sites in order to serve relevant ads. See the "Cookies and advertising" section below for details and opt-out options.

Cookies and advertising

This site is monetized through Google AdSense. As part of that, Google and its partner vendors use cookies — including the DoubleClick cookie — to serve ads based on a visitor's prior visits to this site or other sites on the internet. This may include personalized ads (based on your browsing history) or non-personalized ads (based only on context like the page content and general location), depending on your settings and region.

You can control how Google uses this data for advertising in a few ways:

  • Visit Google's Ad Settings to opt out of personalized advertising or see which advertisers are showing you ads.
  • Visit www.aboutads.info to opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising more broadly.
  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking cookies may affect ad relevance but will not stop ads from being shown, since the site is ad-supported.

Read more about how Google uses data in the context of ads in Google's Advertising Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-party services we use

  • Google AdSense — serves the advertisements on this site.
  • Google Analytics (GA4) & Google Tag Manager — collects aggregate, anonymized traffic and usage statistics.
  • Formspree — processes and delivers submissions from the contact form.
  • Google Fonts — serves the web fonts used for the site's typography.
  • cdnjs (Cloudflare) — serves the React and SheetJS JavaScript libraries that power the calculators; no calculation data is sent to this service, it only hosts the code.

Each of these third parties has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data; we encourage you to review them if you have questions beyond what's covered here.

Data retention

We do not operate our own database of user data. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' standard retention settings. Contact form messages are retained in our email inbox for as long as needed to address your inquiry, and are not sold or shared beyond what's described in this policy.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for a general, business-oriented audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information through the contact form, please reach out and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the tools, services, or regulations that apply to this site. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated whenever changes are made. Continued use of the site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about this policy

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, reach out through our contact page. This policy is provided for transparency and is not a substitute for legal advice.
