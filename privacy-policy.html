Privacy Policy

What we collect, what we don't, and how advertising works on this site.

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The Short Version

Calculate My Hours does not require an account and does not store your timesheet data on any server. Everything you enter — shift times, settings, overtime thresholds — is saved locally in your browser using localStorage and never leaves your device. We do not sell personal data, and we never will. The rest of this page covers the details required by law and by our advertising partners.

Information You Provide Directly

Timesheet data

Shift times, dates, settings, and any labels you enter into the calculator are stored only in your browser's local storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers because we don't operate a backend for the timesheet tool — the entire calculator runs client-side, in your browser, using JavaScript. Clearing your browser's site data or local storage will permanently erase this information; we have no copy of it and cannot recover it for you.

Contact form submissions

If you use the contact form, the name, email address, subject, and message you provide are transmitted to our form-processing provider, Formspree, and forwarded to us by email. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry. We do not add contact-form submitters to a mailing list, and we do not share this information with third parties beyond what's necessary to operate the form (i.e., Formspree's own processing of the submission).

Cookies and Automated Data Collection

This site uses third-party services that may set cookies or collect data automatically when you visit. These services operate independently of our own code and have their own privacy practices:

Google AdSense

We display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data and manage your ad personalization settings at policies.google.com/technologies/ads. If you're in the European Economic Area or UK, ad personalization is subject to the consent choices you make when prompted.

Google Tag Manager

We use Google Tag Manager to load the scripts on this site (including AdSense) in a controlled way. Tag Manager itself is a script-loading tool; it does not collect personal data on its own, but the tags it loads (like AdSense) may.

Your cookie choices

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking cookies may cause some parts of this site — mainly ad display — to function differently, but the timesheet calculator itself will continue to work normally, since it doesn't depend on cookies.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't require registration or a login to use the calculator
  • We don't store your timesheet entries on any server or database
  • We don't sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties
  • We don't track you across other websites beyond what Google's advertising cookies do independently
  • We don't use your contact-form messages for anything other than replying to you

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information through the contact form, please reach out and we'll delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the tools we use or in applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled? Reach out through our contact page and we'll respond directly.
